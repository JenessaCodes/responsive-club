Events

Women's Code & Tea

This is an event for women or femme individuals only. This event is meant to highlight and provide commradery among women in the coding field. Community members will share their journey into coding, provide support to other women embarking in similar paths, and host workshops to expand the presence of women in tech.

Bring a Friend Beginner's Code Day

This is a true-beginner friendly event designed to expose newbies to wonderful world of coding. This event is specifically focused to support someone who has never been taught a line of code. We love this event because it challenges our teachers to design the easiest and most fun workshops and activities to become introduced to code and learn more about it. Previous teachers have said the enjoy the position of being the person they wish they had in the beginning of their careers!

Code & Cook Afternoon

This event brings people to fellowship together by cooking and working on coding projects. It will be a scrumptious and productive day you won't want to miss!

Teach & Learnathon

Small Business Web Developer Community Service Project

This is an event for coders to share their skills by forming teams to create or improve a website for a local small business. A lot of the time our small businesses donate catered meals or services to our hard working volunteers! It's a fun and creative challenge that you can hint: put on your resume! Don't miss this fun giving event!

Location

We will be holding most of our in-person meetings at the Wework Center (Address: 123 Boulder Ave, Oceanside, CA 90210). If a meeting will be held at another location, details will be posted within the event.

Covid Policy

Per state mandate, you will be required to sign our liability waiver and show proof of full vaccination (Booster not required). If you are not vaccinated, you must present a negative covid-test taken within 48 hours prior to the event in which you wish to attend. DIY covid-tests are permitted, but must be taken in-person with one of our community coordinators. (Do NOT take an at-home covid-test and bring us the results as we have no way to verify that it was taken by you. It must be taken on site at our registration desk.)
